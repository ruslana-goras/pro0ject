Discover the beauty of flowers and their meanings

Rose

The rose, originating from Persia, has been a symbol of love and beauty for centuries. Its diverse colors convey various emotions, from deep red for passion to white for purity.

Sunflower

Sunflowers are native to North America and were cultivated by Native Americans as a valuable food source. Their vibrant yellow petals symbolize adoration and loyalty.

Lily

Lilies, with their origins in Asia, are associated with various meanings across cultures. They symbolize purity and majesty and are often linked to religious and historical stories.
